Solar Energy: Harvesting the Sun's Power

Solar energy is radiant light and heat from the Sun harnessed using technologies like photovoltaic cells and solar thermal collectors. It is one of the most abundant and clean energy sources available.

Photovoltaic (PV) cells convert sunlight directly into electricity, which is known as the photovoltaic effect. This method is common for residential and commercial installations.

Solar thermal systems, on the other hand, use the sun's heat to warm fluids, which can then be used to generate steam and drive turbines.
